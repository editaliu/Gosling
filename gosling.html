La La Land (2006)


Here's a time line of Ryan Gosling's life:

  • November 12, 1980: Ryan Gosling is born in Ontario, Canada.
  • 1993: He joins The Mickey Mouse Club.
  • 1995-1999: Gosling stars in a series of family-oriented TV shows.
  • 2000: Gosling snags his first lead role, in Henry Bean's The Believer.
  • 2002-2003: Gosling starts to establish his indie cred.
  • 2004: Gosling stars in The Notebook.
  • 2005–2007: Gosling begins to earn serious critical acclaim for his acting work.
  • 2008: Gosling becomes a meme.
  • 2010-2011: The world reaches Peak Gosling.
  • 2012: Gosling announces his directorial debut, originally titled How to Catch a Monster.
  • 2013: Gosling announces a break from acting.
  • 2014: Gosling’s directorial debut, now titled Lost River, premieres at Cannes.
Canadian actor Ryan Gosling is the first person born in the 1980s to have been nominated for the Best Actor Oscar (for Puse Nelsono (2006)).
